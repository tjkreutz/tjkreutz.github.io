About me

I am a PhD candidate at the Computational Linguistics and Psycholinguistics Research Group (CLiPS) in Antwerp, Belgium. My promotor is Walter Daelemans and I am co-promoted by Peter van Aelst.

My promotors and myself make up three of the nine members on the NWS Data project. The project aim is to investigate how people nowadays inform themselves on actualities, specifically news events and elections. Since new media is at the core of all these interests, challenging NLP problems pop up. I keep myself busy working on them. Since this is also a personal interest blog, I may post some unrelated projects as well.
