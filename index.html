About me

I am a student at the University of Groningen, currently enrolled in the Information Science master programme. My graduation thesis focuses on Natural Language Processing of legal texts. Specifically I use the Open Data van de Rechtspraak data collections to predict applicable laws for court cases. I am scheduled to graduate in July under prof. dr. Gertjan van Noord.

You can already have a look at my bachelor's thesis on news event detection on Twitter here as well as some goofier projects and blog posts below.

Will try to update regularly.
